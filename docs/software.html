Software

Examples were calculated in R version 4.1.0 (2021-05-18) with an effort to use the most recent versions of R packages.

The R packages used in this book include (in alphabetic order):

  • car (Fox et al. 2021)
  • dplyr (Wickham et al. 2022b)
  • Ecdat (Croissant and Graves 2020)
  • fma (Hyndman 2020)
  • forecast (Hyndman et al. 2022)
  • funtimes (Lyubchich and Gel 2022)
  • ggplot2 (Wickham et al. 2022a)
  • lawstat (Gastwirth et al. 2020)
  • lmtest (Hothorn et al. 2022)
  • patchwork (Pedersen 2020)
  • plotly (Sievert et al. 2022)
  • pracma (Borchers 2021)
  • randtests (Caeiro and Mateus 2014)
